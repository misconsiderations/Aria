How to Get Your Token

Use the steps below for your device. Keep your token private and do not share it.

Security warning treat token like password
Important Private
Never paste your token in chats, screenshots, or support tickets.
1

Open Discord or discord.com.

2

Press F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I and open the Console tab.

3

Paste and run this command:

Your token appears in console output.

1

iOS: Create a bookmark and set its URL to javascript:alert(localStorage.getItem('token')).

2

Open discord.com in your mobile browser and sign in.

3

Tap the bookmarklet you created to show the token value.

4

Android alternative: enable USB debugging and inspect from desktop if bookmarklets are blocked.

1

Open Discord Desktop.

2

Press Ctrl+Shift+I and open Console.

3

Run the desktop command and copy token result.
